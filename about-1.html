WHO IS PRIME GATE INTERNATIONAL TRADING LTD

PRIME GATE INTERNATIONAL TRADING LTD Was established in 2025, under the companies Act CAP 2002 of United Republic of Tanzania, with the objectives of providing the best services in Clearing and forwarding and operating Import/Export business internationally. And Authorised customs Agent to offer professional services by Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA) and Tanzania port Authority (TPA) respectively. PRIME GATE INTERNATIONAL TRADING LTD is licensed by the commissioner for customs and Excise Department since its establishment to undertake various kinds of import and export, customs clearances at Dar es salaam, Mtwara,Tanga,Bagamoyo,Mwambani on Indian ocean and other major port, Airports and Borders in an efficient way and trouble-free manner. The company started as Customs clearing Agent and freight forwarding services including Importation, Exportation and Transit. PRIME GATE INTERNATIONAL TRADING LTD has the objectives of providing logistics services, transportation services domestically and internationally worldwide 🌐.
