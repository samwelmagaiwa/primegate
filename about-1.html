WHO IS PRIME GATE INTERNATIONAL TRADING LTD

PRIME GATE INTERNATIONAL TRADING LTD Was established in 2025, under the companies Act CAP 2002 of United Republic of Tanzania, with the objectives of providing the best services in Clearing and forwarding and operating Import/Export business internationally. And Authorised customs Agent to offer professional services by Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA) and Tanzania port Authority (TPA) respectively. PRIME GATE INTERNATIONAL TRADING LTD is licensed by the commissioner for customs and Excise Department since its establishment to undertake various kinds of import and export, customs clearances at Dar es salaam, Mtwara,Tanga,Bagamoyo,Mwambani on Indian ocean and other major port, Airports and Borders in an efficient way and trouble-free manner. The company started as Customs clearing Agent and freight forwarding services including Importation, Exportation and Transit. Vision and Mission and Objectives Statements

Vision

Prime Gate International Ltd vision is To be the first-choice logistics and clearance company, recognized for excellence in port operations and end-to-end transportation solutions across local and international markets.

Mission

To deliver reliable, efficient, and cost-effective logistics, clearance, and transportation services by leveraging industry expertise, modern technology, and strong partnerships, while ensuring compliance, safety, and exceptional customer satisfaction.

Objectives

  • To expand operations and build strong partnerships across local and international markets.
  • To deliver timely cargo clearance and efficient port and transportation services.
  • To provide reliable, transparent, and customer-focused logistics solutions



Primegate is a global logistics leader with strong capabilities in sea freight, air freight, contract logistics, and overland transportation.
